Frequently
asked
questions

Here you can find answers regarding Shopify setups, third-party logistics migrations, and performance speed testing.

What is your migration process to Shopify?

+

We handle complete migrations from WooCommerce, Magento, or custom frameworks. We import products, client records, and SEO URL mappings securely to prevent any drop in organic search engine rankings.

How do you optimize checkout conversion rates?

+

We design streamlined checkout flows, deploy single-page checkouts, integrate digital wallets, and place micro-copy at high-friction fields to build trust and lower cart abandonment.

Do you work with headless commerce architectures?

+

Yes. For high-traffic brands, we construct headless storefronts using Shopify Hydrogen (Remix) or Next.js, linking to Shopify's Storefront API. This yields maximum speeds and creative flexibility.

What support do you provide post-launch?

+

We offer monthly retention retainers covering regular conversion tests, page speed checkups, cart optimizations, app integrations, and new campaign page launches.
